The Best Movies According to Negar

My top 3 movies of all-time.

  1. Harry Potter

    Harry Potter is a fantasy movie series based on the novels by J.K. Rowling. The story follows a young wizard named Harry who discovers his magical heritage on his 11th birthday.

  2. Flex X Cop

    Flex x Cop is a South Korean action-comedy film that follows the story of Jin Yi-soo, a rich and rebellious young man who unexpectedly becomes a detective.

  3. Thunderbolts

    Thunderbolts is an upcoming Marvel movie that brings together a team of anti-heroes and former villains from the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The team includes characters like Yelena Belova, Bucky Barnes (The Winter Soldier), Red Guardian, Taskmaster, U.S. Agent, and Ghost.
